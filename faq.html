Frequently Asked Questions






Are there any fees to send money using SwiftPay®?

SwiftPay® does not charge a fee for money transfers. Please check with your banking institution to ensure there are no fees for SwiftPay® transactions.

How long does it take to receive money with SwiftPay®?

Money sent or received with SwiftPay® is available within minutes. If it has been more than two days, please confirm you have verified your SwiftPay® profile, and that your receiver has a valid email address or U.S. mobile number.
If you still encounter this issue, please reach out to our customer support team for assistance.

What if my bank or financial institution doesn't offer SwiftPay®?

In that case, you should still be able to send and receive money assuming you have the SwiftPay® app installed.

What if my payment hasn't gone through?

If your payment hasn't gone through, and it has been longer than three business days, please reach out to our customer support team.
